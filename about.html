ABOUT

hey i'm kiara (she/her)! i'm from the sf bay area and i shoot photos. i've done it all my life - on shitty flip phones to rare film cameras. i started shooting film in the summer of 2019 and it re-energized my passion for photography and inspired me to make this site after many years of putting it off.

i coded this site from scratch because i was tired of instagram wrecking the resolution of the images i posted, so here we are. you can enjoy my photos in all their glory by clicking them and zooming in to your heart's content. if you want to use my photos for anything, just ask. i have my socials under the 'ontas?' tab.

"phisheye" is a name i use sometimes - it refers to a fisheye lens and the hacker practice of phishing to gain sensitive information from unsuspecting people on the internet.
