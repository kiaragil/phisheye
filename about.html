ABOUT

hey i'm kiara! i'm from the sf bay area and i shoot photos. i've had a camera in my hand my entire life, whether it was shitty flip phone or a high-end dslr. i started shooting film in the summer of 2019 and it re-energized my drive for photography. it also inspired me to create this site after years of putting it off. i coded it from scratch because i was tired of instagram wrecking the resolution of my images. you can enjoy my photos in all their glory by clicking them, zooming, swiping, etc etc. this website is also viewable on a desktop and i highly recommend checking it out at least once. i took a little inspiration from my days on tumblr for the layout.

i have my socials under the 'ontas?' tab if you have any questions for me or if you're wondering how to credit me for use of my photos.

finally, i know you're wondering: "phisheye" is just a name i use sometimes - it's a reference to a fisheye lens and the hacker practice of phishing to gain sensitive information from unsuspecting people on the internet.
